Our Story

Serving Nairobi with crafted flavors and inclusive dining experiences.

Our Journey

Founded in Nairobi’s vibrant Spring Valley, our oven has been the centerpiece of community dining for years. We blend tradition with innovation — halal options, vegan creativity, and a wine list curated for every palate.

Chef’s Philosophy

We believe food should be inclusive, sustainable, and crafted with care. Our chefs source local produce and combine it with global techniques to create dishes that honor Nairobi’s diversity.
